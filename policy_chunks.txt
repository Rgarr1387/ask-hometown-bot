
Hometown Dental provides the following paid time off benefit to its full-time hourly and salary employees:
After one year, employees earn 40 hours of paid time off.
After two years of employment, employees earn 80 hours of paid time off.
-----
Dress Code: Scrubs shall be in the color family of blue or black. Front desk employees can wear scrubs or dress business casual.
-----
Full-time staff is generally scheduled to be onsite for eight (8) hours a day, five days a week.
-----
All full-time staff working a minimum of 30 hours per week are eligible to participate in the health insurance plan.
-----
Clinical employees are required to wear matching scrubs and optional lab jackets along with the appropriate gloves, masks and eyewear mandated by OSHA.
